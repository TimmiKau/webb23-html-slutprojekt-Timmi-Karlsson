...Det som rör oss...

Award winning production “När vi lyser för varandra” of Guldkorn 2019.

""Det som rör oss - ett performativt laboratorium i teckenspråkets universum"
is the second scenic production within the Arvsfond project "... när vi lyser för varandra ..." The purpose of the project is to create curiosity for Swedish sign language and increase the participation of sign language of children and youth within the cultural life. It’s a visual, tactile and physical journey of discovery for visitors within magical rooms.

Det som rör oss – ett performativt laboratorium, has been developed through close collaboration processeses with children and educators at the special department at Uroxen preschool in Lund, families at DHB Södra and participants at the Mo Gård Åkarp folkhögskola. With support from: Allmänna arvsfonden, Statens kulturråd, Kultur Skåne, Lunds kommun, Sparbanksstiftelsen Finn.
